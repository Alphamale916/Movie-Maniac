TOP 5 MOVIES

1. The Shawshank Redemption

Get busy living, or get busy dying.

Andy Dufresne, a successful banker, is arrested for the murders of his wife and her lover, and is sentenced to life imprisonment at the Shawshank prison. He becomes the most unconventional prisoner.

2. The Godfather

Don't Tell Me You're Innocent.

Don Vito Corleone, head of a mafia family, decides to hand over his empire to his youngest son, Michael. However, his decision unintentionally puts the lives of his loved ones in grave danger.

3. The Dark Knight

Why so Serious?

Batman has a new foe, the Joker, who is an accomplished criminal hell-bent on decimating Gotham City. Together with Gordon and Harvey Dent, Batman struggles to thwart the Joker before it is too late.

4. 12 Angry Men

If you want to vote not guilty, then do it because you're convinced the man is not guilty - not because you've had enough.

An eighteen-year-old Latino is accused of having stabbed his father to death. He is presented in a courtroom before a twelve-man jury. Eleven out of the twelve men vote guilty, except for Mr. Davis.

5. Schindler's List

Power - is when we have every justification to kill, and we don't.

Oscar Schindler, a successful and narcissistic German businessman, slowly starts worrying about the safety of his Jewish workforce after witnessing their persecution in Poland during World War II.
